Frequently Ask Questions

Where do you get the recipes?

Our website is integrated with the Spoonacular API. They have over 360,000 recipes made up of over 2600 ingredients.

How is this tool unique?

FoodFinder matches you with the recipes you can make with what you already have on hand, and even takes into account your personal dietary needs and restrictions.

What is the purpose of this project?

We wanted to show the capabilities of the Spoonacular API and the use of different types of data stores.

What technologies did we use?

The front end was made using HTML and the Bulma CSS Framework. The backend uses the provided COMP 426 backend and the Spoonacular API.
